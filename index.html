About Keyboardrop

Keyboardrop is a simple clipboard helper that lets you collect the text, URLs, and images you copy as temporary “drops,” then paste them — in order — into chats, notes, browsers, and AI apps from the built-in keyboard. Add from the share sheet, see URL previews, hand off by QR, and let drops auto-clear over time. No account, no data collection; everything stays on your device. For iPhone and iPad.
